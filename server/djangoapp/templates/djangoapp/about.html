About Us

Unveiling Best Cars:
A Legacy of Excellence, Loyal Service, and Unmatched Automotive Experience.


Founded in 2003, Best Cars dealership transitioned from trade to retail, evolving into a multi-generational family business with over 30 staff members. Our unwavering commitment to exceptional customer service has fostered a loyal customer base, with more than half returning for our services. We take pride in our 'Highly rated' status on Auto Trader and 'Top rated' recognition on Car Gurus.

Our extensive inventory of 400 cars encompasses a diverse array of styles, complemented by our comprehensive facility featuring a workshop, valet bay, and 'smart' repair center. The collective expertise of our staff, amounting to over 130 years in the motor trade, ensures valuable insights for our customers. Many team members have been with us for years, ensuring a warm and familiar atmosphere during each visit.

Best Cars gained prominence when featured in the acclaimed racing movie "Rush" in 2009, highlighting the allure of our vehicles. With prices spanning from $3,000 to $150,000, our wide-ranging selection accommodates various budgets. In need of financing? Our proficiency extends to all credit ratings, with partnerships involving major companies and brokers.

Concerned about warranties? Rest assured, most of our stock comes with up to 4 years of Trading Standards Approved coverage, backed by repair centers nationwide. At Best Cars, we're not solely in the business of selling cars; we're dedicated to delivering an unparalleled customer experience.
